Profile

A graphic designer / web developer based out of Charlotte, NC with over 15 years of experience in graphic design. I have been interested in the arts all of my life. Ever since being a child to the present day, I have dabbled in all kinds of art - finger painting, macaroni necklaces, drawing, painting, sculpting, music, writing, acting, and most recently computer graphics. I am looking for my next big career move with the introduction of web design to my repertoire.

Work + Play

There is a thin line between work and play for me. I love being able to use my creativity while working or having fun. Since most of my hobbies are based from different art forms, graphic and digital design seemed like a perfect fit for me in my career.
